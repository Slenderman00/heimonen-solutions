The lab

IN-HOUSE
Everything gets prototyped and tested here before it goes anywhere near a client's boat, platform, or network. No third-party lab, no waiting on someone else's bench time.
Our flagship scope, a Rohde & Schwarz RTO1024, lets us take on state-of-the-art telecommunications work: measuring high-bandwidth differential pairs in real time, down to the signal integrity of individual lanes.
Through our automated test setups we can recreate and measure almost any phenomenon found in the field. That extends to pre-compliance testing, so problems are caught on our bench, not in certification.
